# PHASE 7 — COMMERCE, PAYMENTS, AND LOYALTY (Sub-prompt)

You are now in PHASE 7 — COMMERCE, PAYMENTS, AND LOYALTY for the Bulk Gym MOBILE APP.

Focus on these master features:

- In-app store for merchandise and supplements
- Membership purchases
- Auto-renew subscriptions
- One-time purchases
- Discounts and promo codes
- Payment history
- Loyalty points and referrals

Your goals:

1. In-App Store
   - Design:
     - A shopping experience specifically for merchandise and supplements.
     - Product listing, product detail, cart, and checkout screens.
   - Keep the scope limited to the master list and do not introduce other product types.

2. Membership and Subscriptions
   - Define:
     - Flows for purchasing and upgrading memberships inside the app.
     - Auto-renew management, including viewing status, canceling, and changing plans.
   - Explain how membership changes reflect in membership status from earlier phases.

3. One-Time Purchases and Discounts
   - Design:
     - User experience for one-time purchases such as passes and add-ons that fit the feature set.
     - Applying discounts and promo codes during checkout.

4. Payment History
   - Describe:
     - How members view their payment history.
     - How different payment types such as membership, one-time, and store purchases are displayed and filtered.

5. Loyalty Points and Referrals
   - Define:
     - How members earn loyalty points and see their balance.
     - Referral flows for sharing referral codes or links and seeing rewards.
   - Tie this into challenges, badges, and the store where appropriate.

6. Output Format
   - Return:
     - Screen and flow descriptions for store, payments, and loyalty.
     - A data model outline for orders, payments, discounts, loyalty, and referrals.
     - A short explanation of how payments integrate with existing membership and booking logic.

Do not build any admin or reporting user interface and stay fully within the consumer mobile scope.
